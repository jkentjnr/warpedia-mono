Voice. The wordmark is set in monospace — JetBrains Mono — to read as a ledger header or accession stamp, not an encyclopedia. The black filing-tab block carrying WP is the hardest point of contrast; oxide rules sit underneath as ledger lines. The mark drops the serif W entirely: a black tab with a tag-hole notch above and an accession-number slug below.

Why monospace, not serif. Wikipedia, Britannica and the entire reference-brand category use a humanist serif wordmark with an underline. We deliberately do not. The serif still appears throughout the product — in body copy and in display headlines — but the brand mark sits in the archival/ledger register instead.
